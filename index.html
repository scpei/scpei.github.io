I am now a Research Associate at King Abdullah Univeristy of Science and Technology since Jan. 2022, working with Prof. Xiangliang Zhang (Univeristy of Notre Dame). I received my Ph.D. at KAUST. Before that, I was a Ph.D. student in computer science at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST) from Jan. 2018 to Dec. 2021. I was a member of the Machine Intelligence and kNowledge Engineering (MINE) Lab in the Computational Bioscience Research Center (CBRC), advised by Prof. Xiangliang Zhang. I received my M.S. degree in computer science from KAUST in December 2017. Before that, I obtained B.Eng. in software engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University in China in July 2016, where my research began under the supervision of Prof. Chao Shen.

My research interests broadly lie in data mining and machine learning, in particular graph representation learning, knowledge representation learning and reasoning, knowledge acquisition and integration, recommendation system, weakly supervised learning, etc.
